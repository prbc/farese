Welcome to Farese,

an online directory of Reformed Baptist churches.

About

This directory of Reformed Baptist churches is a collection of church listings that hold to the 1689 London Baptist Confession of Faith or similar statement of faith. It is our desire that this list would be used of God to give serious-minded Christians an alternative place of worship while on vacation or travel.

Quick Disclaimer

We do not personally know all of these churches nor can we recommend them from personal knowledge. Some of the churches listed in this directory may not be considered by all to be distinctly Reformed. However, they would be like-minded in several areas of faith and practice and, therefore, are listed on this directory.
