About Johnny Farese

Johnny Farese was born in 1956 to a family in Boston, and was raised a Roman Catholic. In his teen years, a jarring move to Florida and visits from a Christian aquaintance gave rise to some spiritual thoughts, even leading to praying the sinner's prayer. In this case, it wasn't accompanied by a God-given change of heart. After bad influence from new friends Johnny involved himself in a life of gambling, sex, and drug abuse.
