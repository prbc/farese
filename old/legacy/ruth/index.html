Earthen Vessels is a collection of devotional writings by Mrs. Ruth Holmes. Ruth has also written a book, yet to be published, entitled "Oh Man, Know Thy Enemies".  She has led numerous womens home Bible studies, labored in missions work and has overcome, by the grace of God, tremendous adversity throughout her life's journey.  She has been a true friend and great encouragement to me over the past 17 years.  

--Johnny Farese (1999)
